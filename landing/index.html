About Us

SmileCraft Dental provides a full range of dental services. Besides general dentistry, our services include dental implants and restorative crowns using the highest quality materials available, periodontics, endodontics, orthodontics and extractions. And, because of the importance of good oral hygiene in maintaining overall health, our office teams include trained dental hygienists who provide complete oral hygiene services.
