Project Overview

I have actively contributed to the creation of a dynamic and engaging web page for the annual HTW IMI Showtime event at HTW Berlin. This event provides students with the platform to present the IT projects they have worked on throughout the entire practical semester. In developing this page, I harnessed the capabilities of Hugo as the static site generator. The main goal was to present project details, highlight participants, and showcase results in a visually appealing and easily navigable format.

Technology Stack

As a web developer, my responsibilities encompassed designing and implementing the comprehensive structure of the project page. This involved incorporating various content types, including text, videos, and images, throughout the staging and production phases, ultimately leading to a successful release. The project capitalized on the versatility, speed, and simplicity offered by Hugo as its static site generator. Leveraging Hugo facilitated efficient content organization, streamlined updates, and seamless deployment.

Design and User Experience

The page design was strategically crafted to create an immersive user experience, with a keen focus on intuitive navigation and visually appealing sections. The layout was meticulously designed to guide users through an engaging journey, ensuring easy exploration of the showcased projects. Interactive elements and captivating visuals were integrated to enhance user interaction and leave a lasting impression on visitors, contributing to the overall success of the HTW IMI Showtime event.

Results and Impact

The newly created Hugo-powered page significantly contributed to the success of IMI Showtime. The page experienced increased user engagement, and positive feedback highlighted the ease of navigation and the seamless presentation of event information.
