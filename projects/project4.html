ROA Remote Onboarding App

Figma UX/UI UX Research


Concept

ROA - the Onboarding App for a Strong "We-Feeling"


The main goal of the app is remote integration into a new work community and getting to know a new environment. This is achieved through a sense of social belonging, customized mentoring, and group membership based on common interests. The central idea is to develop a company integration app for new employees that helps them connect on a social level. The focus is on the Hobby Matcher, where employees can enter their hobbies and preferences, find potential interest partners, connect with them, and exchange ideas. This helps identify commonalities that form a good basis for establishing a personal connection.

Features of the App:


Hobby Matcher: Employees can enter their hobbies and preferences to find potential interest partners and connect with them.
Get-to-Know Meetings with New Colleagues: The app facilitates meetings and interactions among new colleagues to foster connections.
Coffee Chats: A feature to encourage informal communication and bonding among team members.
Find and Connect with a Mentor: The app includes a mentor-finding feature, connecting new employees with mentors who share similar interests.

Target Audience:

The app is designed for both new and existing employees. For new employees, it aims to facilitate social integration into the company, while for existing employees, HR, or managers, it offers tools to quickly integrate newcomers into work processes and the social life of the company.

What ROA Offers - Content Modules & Features


Registration Form: Users need to register to create a profile for individualized exchange.
Personalize Profile: Users can customize their profiles with personal information, avatars, and interests.
News Feed (Homepage): Displays company news and updates.
Content List Page: Shows all available Hobby Rooms.
Create a New Room: Allows users to create new hobby/interest rooms.
Join a Room: Users can join existing rooms to engage with colleagues.
Overview of All Employees in the Room: Provides an overview of colleagues sharing similar interests.
Create and Engage in Posts & Events: Users can create and participate in interest-specific posts and events.
Mentor Section: Lists available mentors based on shared interests and departments.
Search for a Mentor: Allows users to find mentors with similar interests.
Messenger Section: Enables personal and group messaging for quick communication.

ROA, focuses on social integration, offering a unique approach to connect employees based on shared interests, fostering a stronger sense of unity within the company.
